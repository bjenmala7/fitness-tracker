# 💪 Fitness Tracker - 69kg to 50kg Journey

A complete, free, open-source fitness tracking web app for managing your weight loss journey. Track calories, meals, workouts, and progress with **AI-powered food recognition**!

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Active-brightgreen)
![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-blue)
![Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/Version-2.0-orange)

---

## ✨ **Features**

### 📊 **Calorie Tracking**
- Daily calorie intake monitoring
- Track calories eaten vs. burned
- Real-time calorie balance calculation
- Daily deficit tracking

### ⚖️ **Weight Progress**
- Log weight entries with dates
- Visual progress tracking (69kg → 50kg)
- Milestone markers (65kg, 60kg, 55kg, 50kg)
- Progress percentage bar

### 📸 **Smart Meal Logging**
- **AI-Powered Image Recognition** - Upload food photos, Claude AI analyzes instantly
- **Weight Scale Detection** - AI reads weight from scale in photos
- **Automatic Calorie Calculation** - Gets exact calories based on portion weight
- **Nutrition Breakdown** - Protein, carbs, fat, and fiber display
- Preset meals for quick logging
- Custom meal entries
- Photo storage with each meal

### 🏋️ **Workout Tracking**
- **Morning Yoga** (60 min, 180 cal)
  - 12 guided yoga poses
  - Video tutorials for each asana
  - Countdown timers
  
- **Evening Intense Workout** (60 min, 350 cal)
  - 16 exercises with sets/reps
  - YouTube video guides
  - Real-time timer
  
- Day-by-day tracking (Mon-Fri)
- Progress bars per day

### 📈 **Progress Analytics**
- **Daily View** - Last 30 days activity log
- **Monthly Summary** - Total workouts, weight lost, avg calories
- **6-Month Progress** - Comprehensive journey overview
- Achievement badges (1kg lost, 5kg lost, 10+ workouts, 30+ active days)
- Detailed statistics:
  - Average meals per day
  - Average calories per day
  - Total meals logged
  - Consistency percentage
- Visual weight loss charts

### 💾 **Data Management**
- Everything saves **locally** (browser storage)
- No login needed
- No data sent to servers
- Works completely offline
- Export-friendly format

## 📱 **How to Use**

### **Calories Tab**
1. Log meals using quick add or custom entry
2. See real-time calorie balance
3. Track daily deficit for weight loss

### **Weight Tab**
1. Enter your current weight
2. Watch progress toward 50kg goal
3. See milestones as you reach them

### **Meals Tab**
1. Upload food photo (with weight scale if possible)
2. Enter meal name
3. Click "Analyze 🤖" for AI calorie calculation
4. Or manually enter calories
5. Save meal with photo

### **Workouts Tab**
1. Select a day (Mon-Fri)
2. Complete yoga asanas (6:30-7:30 AM)
3. Complete workout exercises (5:30-6:30 PM)
4. Click each exercise for video guide & timer
5. Check them off as you complete

### **Progress Tab**
1. **Daily** - See last 30 days of meals
2. **Monthly** - Monthly stats & weight trend
3. **6 Months** - Full journey with achievements & detailed stats
